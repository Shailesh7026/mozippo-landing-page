Home Privacy Policy

Welcome to Mozippo! Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how Mozippo collects, uses, shares, and protects your information. By using Mozippo, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account Information: When you create an account, we collect your name, email address, date of birth, gender, and profile picture.
  • Profile Details: You can provide additional information about yourself, such as your bio, interests, and photos.
  • Usage Data: We collect data on how you use Mozippo, such as your interactions with other users, the features you use, and your activity patterns.
  • Location Data: With your permission, we collect your location to show you nearby matches.
  • Device Information: We collect information about the device you use to access Mozippo, including the device type, operating system, and unique device identifiers.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To Provide and Improve Mozippo: We use your information to operate, maintain, and improve the app and its features.
  • To Match You with Others: Your profile information and preferences help us suggest potential matches.
  • To Communicate with You: We use your email to send you important updates, notifications, and promotional messages (you can opt out of these).
  • To Ensure Safety: We use your information to monitor activity on the app, detect fraud, and enforce our Terms of Use.
  • To Personalize Your Experience: We use your data to customize your experience and show you relevant content.

3. How We Share Your Information

  • With Other Users: Your profile information is visible to other users of the app. Be mindful of the information you choose to share.
  • With Service Providers: We share your data with third-party service providers who help us operate and improve Mozippo. These providers are bound by confidentiality agreements.
  • For Legal Reasons: We may share your information if required by law or to protect our rights and safety, or the rights and safety of others.
  • In Case of Business Transfers: If Mozippo undergoes a business transition, such as a merger or acquisition, your information may be transferred as part of that process.

4. Your Choices

  • Profile Visibility: You can control what information is visible on your profile and adjust your privacy settings within the app.
  • Location Sharing: You can enable or disable location sharing at any time through your device settings.
  • Communication Preferences: You can opt out of promotional emails by following the unsubscribe instructions in the email.

5. Data Security

  • Protection Measures: We implement security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, loss, or misuse. However, no system is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee the absolute security of your data.
  • Account Security: Keep your account credentials private and report any unauthorized access immediately.

6. Data Retention

  • Storage Duration: We retain your information as long as necessary to provide our services and fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy. You can request deletion of your account and data at any time.

7. Children's Privacy

  • Age Restriction: Mozippo is not intended for users under 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 18. If we become aware of such data, we will delete it promptly.

8. Changes to This Policy

  • Updates: We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes and update the effective date at the top of the policy.

9. Contact Us

  • Questions and Concerns: If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us through the app or at support@mozippo.com.

By using Mozippo, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy and agree to our data practices. Thank you for trusting us with your information. Happy dating!
